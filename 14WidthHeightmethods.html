Let's use all kind of width and height method in jQuery.

  • width()
  • innerWidth() - Take width counting from content to padding
  • outerWidth() - Take width counting from content to border
  • outerWidth(true) - Take width counting from content to margin
  • height()
  • innerHeight() - Take counting from content to padding
  • outerHeight() - Take counting from content to border
  • outerHeight(true) - Take counting from content to margin
  • hasClass() method is used to find whether the tag has any class attach or not.
  • scrollLeft() and scrollTop() use to scroll the page in their specific direction.
